DotApprove is particularly relevant to those organisations that require a rigorous method of storing electronic assets and collateral and recording their approval records for compliance purposes.

We have numerous security features in place to keep your information safe both on and offline. These include our world-renowned hosting provider and intelligent encryption facilities.

DotApprove also protects you against any loss of access to information if your internal systems go down, helping with business continuity and preventing you from losing vital pieces of work.

DotApprove is used by many leading financial and professional services firms and other regulated companies. Security is therefore the number one priority, which is why we selected Rackspace, one of the world's leading hosting providers to be our partner.

Rackspace offers exacting, rigorous standards and delivers unparalleled security, power, connectivity and environmental control.

If you'd like to know more about our security, please contact us.

Information Security

Information Security is of critical importance to Perivan due to the nature of our business activities, and the trust placed in us by our clients when we handle their extremely confidential data. We therefore have a very strong security awareness culture that permeates every management and staff level at Perivan.

Our working practices conform to ISO27001 standards and our processes are audited. We are working towards extending our ISO27001 Certificate to encompass DotApprove.
