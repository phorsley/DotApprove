Perivan is serious in its commitment to fairness in the workplace, care for the environment, health and safety, security management, diversity, sustainability and corporate responsibility. We are constantly striving to improve our performance in these areas.

We are strong believers in ethical buying. Our dealings with our suppliers are straightforward and considerate. We demand a high level of performance from our suppliers but work in partnership to help them to achieve it. We believe well managed, efficient and hardworking suppliers should be able to make a profit.

Please contact us for more information or copies of any of the following policies:

  • Environmental
  • Equal opportunities
  • Health and safety
  • Data protection
  • Information security
  • Business continuity

Environment and ISO 14001

Perivan is proud to have installed an ISO 14001 Environmental Management System, and were first registered to this standard by ISOQAR in May 2004. Perivan's serious commitment to the environment has been demonstrated in the growth of the scope of our EMS over the last years.
