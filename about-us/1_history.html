DotApprove was developed in response to clients' frustration with the way they collate comments and sign off on new marketing material.

For many, the process was time consuming and laborious and inefficiencies meant they were exposed to breaching regulations such as the Financial Conduct Authority.

Our vision is to continue streamlining the process of project managing, reviewing and approving all documentation, marketing collateral and social media. The amazing results we've achieved in automating processes that improve compliance and brand consistency, reduce administration and risk and significantly save time and money are what motivate us.

We are very excited about the possibilities for adding new features and continuing to resolve clients' problems.

The team behind DotApprove has many years' experience in designing and developing software and web applications for financial and professional services firms. We're part of Perivan Limited who were established in 1937 and now employ over 70 staff based at our London and Edinburgh offices.

The company is 100% owned by its employees, which helps foster a positive, proactive culture. We have over 250 clients worldwide and the financial muscle to continually invest in product development.

You may also be interested in our other software products such as MarketingStore; an online portal for creating and ordering marketing collateral from your approved suppliers, and Virtualboardroom; which automates the compilation and distribution of board papers and improves board communication.
