We are seen by our clients as the solution that brings their teams together to collaborate, review and approve all their collateral. Don't take our word for it, read what our clients have to say.

DotApprove is a web-based compliance portal that brings marketing and compliance teams together to collaborate over, approve and project manage all their documents and marketing material quickly and efficiently. It streamlines the sign off process, eradicating paper trails which are easily lost or mislaid and enforces the firm's compliance procedures. It retains a permanent audit trail and material is easily located and retrieved to meet with the FCA regulations and requirements. It also ensures our brand, tone and content is consistently applied across the organisation irrespective of the publication or distribution location. As a result of its success the product has been rolled out to all our offices and is also being reviewed by other business areas to help them meet their objectives. F&C marketing and compliance believes DotApprove would enhance any firm's marketing processes and compliance procedures.

Julia Thomson, Senior Compliance Officer, F&C Asset Management

DotApprove has transformed our internal marketing approval process. It is extremely easy to use! Reviewers are prompted via email, which provides them a link to the relevant online folder required for sign off. This gives quick and easy access so they can review and approve marketing material. Before, we spent so much time chasing people to sign off marketing material, but DotApprove automatically reminds reviewers if they are coming close to a deadline which is saving us lots of time. Jobs go through far less proof stages because reviewers can now open and comment on one central file, and instantly see everyone else's comments. So much time used to be wasted deciphering handwriting, collating everyone's comments which then had to be sent back to our agency. With DotApprove this is now already done for us clearly and concisely, so now we get the new proofs back from our agency much faster! We are also saving hundreds of pounds every month on printing costs. There is no need for filing cabinets full of paper job bags anymore and finding archived jobs is much easier with the search option. I would highly recommend DotApprove to any marketing, compliance and PR teams. Everyone at Perivan has been so helpful with the implementation and changes have been made to tailor our needs. No ask is too much!
