With automated sign-off stages and centralised collaboration DotApprove will make your approval process more efficient and effective.

Whether you are checking marketing collateral for brand consistency, ensuring content meets your Treating Customers Fairly rules or managing the compliance approval of your financial promotions, DotApprove will streamline your processes saving significant time and money. Different approval workflows can be set up for each type of collateral including checklists, ensuring that vital steps aren't missed out.

Any type of collateral can be reviewed and approved including websites, blogs, emails, Twitter messages, PDF's and Microsoft files, which are stored in DotApprove to meet regulatory requirements. Reviewers add their comments and changes to centrally stored files, reducing the likelihood of errors, duplication and conflicting changes. This efficient review process reduces the number of proof stages and enables you to control agency input and bring down their costs.

Create pre-defined approval workflows for all your collateral types and review stages to ensure all required steps are completed.

Email instructions to single or multiple reviewers with links to files stored centrally in DotApprove.

Reviewers can collaborate with colleagues and add their comments and changes to centrally stored files. They can also check back through previous proofs to see the review history.
