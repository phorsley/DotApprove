DotApprove reduces your risk of breaching regulations by automatically archiving all collateral and creating an audit trail of every proof and sign-off stage.

The audit trail provides a complete version history, so you can refer back to previous stages, reviewer's comments, check list answers and approval signatures. All collateral and financial promotions are stored electronically in their original format including working snapshots of websites, so you can keep a record of how your website appeared on a specific date. The powerful search facility allows you to scan through the entire system and within document content. This is particularly useful when specific details such as disclaimers need to be updated.

DotApprove makes it simple to prepare for and quickly respond to requests for information or audits by regulators such as the FCA, PRA, FINRA or SEC.

You can also set up expiry dates and email alerts, so that you can plan your work load efficiently and ensure that all company compliance deadlines are met.
