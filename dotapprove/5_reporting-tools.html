DotApprove's reporting tools give you instant access to valuable marketing and management information.

Whether you want to analyse your marketing campaigns, see how many bids you've won or lost or know how many approvals the compliance department have completed in the last month, DotApprove allows you to view and collate the information immediately.

Reports can be viewed graphically on screen or exported in a variety of formats for integration with other systems. Any data that is captured within DotApprove can be incorporated within your reports including job briefing information, timelines, approval workflows, checklist answers, issue logs and user details.

DotApprove gives you the ability to find out where delays are occurring and identify common issues so that you can implement continual improvements and process efficiencies throughout your business.

Select the report that you require and enter your filter criteria such as date range, user name, approval stages or collateral type. The results are instantly displayed on screen. You can adjust the filter details and re-run the reports as required.
