DotApprove's digital asset management software helps you to easily store and share assets across your business.

It allows you to create global and localised libraries to centralise access for all your brand assets including guidelines, logos, banners, images, videos, Microsoft templates and for legal assets such as policy documents and disclaimers. In fact any file (or asset) you can think of can be securely stored and shared with your staff or third parties such as agencies.

It's quick and easy to search, edit and download files as you need them and a version history and audit trail is automatically captured every time you update a file.

The asset library can be extended using our content and slide library tools allowing staff to browse and insert approved content into their documents and presentations when using Microsoft Word or PowerPoint.
