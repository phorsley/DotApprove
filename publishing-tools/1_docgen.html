Used by some of the world's leading financial firms, DocGen's automated publishing tools will help you generate your documents quickly and accurately.

Updating and producing documents like factsheets, KIID's, client reports or PowerPoint presentations can be complex and time consuming. But with DocGen you can entirely automate the process, from importing data and content to translation and approval, making it quick and simple.

Hundreds of documents can be instantly published in your corporate style, reducing timescales and costs, freeing up time and improving speed to market.

Content is linked to templates that we create to ensure every document is accurate and brand consistent.

Your team can review and approve documents and retain an audit trail and archive to meet regulatory requirements.

Import performance data from internal or external sources and update any text content such as fund manager commentaries.

Your data and content is securely stored in DocGen where it can be project managed and reviewed using sign-off workflows.

Select which documents you want to create. DocGen will import the approved content into the design template and instantly publish accurate and brand consistent documents. If any changes need to be made simply update the content and regenerate your documents.

Once approved all documents, source files and an audit trail of every proof and sign-off stage can be archived to meet FCA regulations.
