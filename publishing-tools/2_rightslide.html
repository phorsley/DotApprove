How to track changes in PowerPoint

Download Our Guide

Our presentation management software gives you all the tools you need to control brand consistency and access a searchable library of presentations, slides and images ensuring the most up-to-date and compliant content is used across your business.

RightSlide includes PowerPoint template tools to guarantee the correct fonts, colours, styles and designs are used while branded charts, graphs and tables can be instantly created and inserted from Excel or the chart library. Your presenters will have 24/7 access (online and offline) to a library of the latest presentations and the ability to search and select pre-approved slides, images and videos.

Any new or edited slides can contain a "not approved" watermark, forcing presenters to get any bespoke content signed-off before their presentation. Workflows within DotApprove allow reviewers to collaborate and approve slides before they're released to the library.

When slides (such as fund performance charts) are amended and published to the library all presentations that contain those slides are automatically updated saving significant time while ensuring the latest content is always used. Users will even be prompted when opening locally saved presentations of any slides that have been updated and can use the compare feature to select which slides to refresh.

RightSlide helps even inexperienced PowerPoint users to create beautiful, on-brand and compliant presentations quickly and easily.

  1. We create branded templates to lock down your slide masters, fonts, colours and content styles and develop themes for your charts, graphs and tables.
  2. You can create central libraries for presentations, commonly used slides, images and video files. Decide whether slides are locked or editable by users. Enter descriptions and meta data so that users can easily search and find specific slides.
  3. Users can access the latest presentations and browse (including offline) and insert pre-approved slides, charts, images and videos using our PowerPoint plug in. A film strip of selected slides is displayed allowing users to drag and drop into the required order.
  4. Old presentations and slides including locally saved copies can be instantly updated when the latest versions are published to the library.
