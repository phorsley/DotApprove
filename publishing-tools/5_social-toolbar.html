DotApprove gives firms the ability to embrace social media with the peace of mind that online conversations are compliant, on brand and fully archived.

DotApprove's Social Toolbar for Twitter has been developed to help you comply with regulations.

It gives companies the ability to manage all their corporate Twitter accounts from one central online dashboard ensuring any financial promotions are clear, fair and not misleading.

The key benefits are the level of control and the archiving and audit trail that it gives to financial firms. You decide which Twitter accounts an employee can view and post to and which messages have to be approved by compliance.

Users can view timelines, search for hashtags or key words, monitor mentions and direct messages and keep track of followers and who you follow.

Automated workflows ensure messages are approved promptly and an audit trail of all comments, changes and approval signatures is automatically captured.

All messages including responses to customer service queries are archived to meet regulatory requirements. When the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) come to audit, simply search to find your Tweet and view the audit trail to prove the correct approval process was followed.

Set up your content libraries in DotApprove and define user access. This allows you to create global libraries that are visible to all users and local libraries, which can be restricted by office locations or business divisions.

Control which Twitter accounts an employee can view and post messages to and decide whether all their tweets must be checked before they're posted or if only financial promotions require sign-off.

Automated workflows ensure prompt sign-off and allow reviewers to check content and responses to tweeted questions and either reject or approve messages

All Twitter messages including the approval audit trail are archived to meet FCA regulations and can be quickly be searched to find specific information.
