DotApprove gives firms the ability to embrace social media with the peace of mind that online conversations are compliant and on brand.

DotApprove's Social Toolbar for Twitter has been developed to help you comply with regulations.

It gives companies the ability to manage all their corporate Twitter accounts from one central online dashboard ensuring any financial promotions are clear, fair and not misleading.

The key benefit is the level of control that it gives to financial firms. You decide which Twitter accounts an employee can view and post to and which messages have to be approved by compliance.

Users can view timelines, search for hashtags or key words, monitor mentions and direct messages and keep track of followers and who you follow.

Automated workflows ensure messages are approved promptly and an audit trail of all comments, changes and approval signatures is automatically captured.

All messages and version histories are archived to meet regulatory requirements. When the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) come to audit, simply search to find your Tweet and view the audit trail to prove the correct approval process was followed.
