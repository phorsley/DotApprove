Our easy to use templates work with all versions of Microsoft Office, ensuring brand consistency across your business.

Perfect for stationery and client facing documents like proposals, presentations and reports, our Microsoft template software will lock down your corporate style, helping you and your team create professional documents that are brand consistent and accurate without needing a designer.

Templates can include tools for controlling fonts, layout and colour palettes. Branded charts and tables can be created in a click, while compliant information (such as disclaimers, biographies or staff photos) can be inserted from the DotApprove content library straight into your documents.

Whether you're working in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint or Excel you can concentrate on writing content and adding value and let our templates take care of everything else.

Document templates and tools for inserting content and controlling style and colour palettes can be accessed directly from the Microsoft ribbon.

Layout, fonts and colour themes are locked by the template to protect your corporate style.

Branded charts and tables can be instantly created and inserted into your Word or PowerPoint files by highlighting the data and selecting the style from the ribbon.
