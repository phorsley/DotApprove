Centrally manage, approve and store your presentations and slides ensuring the most up-to-date, compliant and on brand content is used across your business.

DotApprove's online slide library gives your presenters 24/7 access to the latest presentations allowing them to quickly and easily browse and insert pre-approved slides, charts, images and videos using our PowerPoint plugin.

Old presentations and slides are instantly updated with the latest versions so you know the most up-to-date content is used. Any new or edited slides contain a "not approved" watermark, forcing presenters to get any bespoke content signed-off before their presentation.

Administrators can manage libraries and content and define whether slides are locked or editable. Workflows allow reviewers to sign-off bespoke content and collaborate and approve slides before they're released to the library.
