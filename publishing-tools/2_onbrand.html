Our easy to use templates work with all versions of Microsoft Office, ensuring brand consistency across your business.

Perfect for stationery and client facing documents like proposals, presentations and reports, our OnBrand Microsoft template software will lock down your corporate style, helping you and your team create professional documents that are brand consistent and accurate without needing a designer.

Templates can include tools for controlling fonts, layout and colour palettes while data can be imported to automate content creation such as for research documents or factsheets. Branded charts and tables can be created in a click and pre-approved content (such as disclaimers, biographies or staff photos) can be inserted using the SmartShare content library straight into your documents. We can even provide you with tools to instantly publish content in a variety of formats including web pages, HTML emails and PDF's direct from your templates.

Whether you're working in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint or Excel you can concentrate on writing content and adding value and let our templates take care of everything else.

Document templates and tools for inserting content and controlling style and colour palettes can be accessed directly from the Microsoft ribbon.

Layout, fonts and colour themes are locked by the template to protect your corporate style.

Branded charts and tables can be instantly created and inserted into your Word or PowerPoint files by highlighting the data and selecting the style from the ribbon.
