Project Management

Project manage all marketing collateral, financial promotions, PR campaigns and documentation across multiple departments, office locations and suppliers from a central online system.

Online dashboards

Instant dashboard view of all projects, timelines and approval stages with on screen filters that are customised to your requirements.

Project briefing

Auto generate job numbers and capture consistent project briefs using online forms tailored to your needs including text, date and numerical fields, drop down menu’s, check boxes and multiple choice selections.

Approval workflows

Customise the approval process for each type of collateral and enforce sign-off stages to ensure vital steps aren’t missed.

Approval signatures

Record of all approval signatures.

Online proofing and review

Reviewers can annotate centrally stored proofs and files and collaborate in real time by viewing comments and changes from their colleagues.

Version history

Revised versions of files are automatically saved allowing users to look back through previous proofs to see what revisions were made.

Audit trail

All comments, changes, checklist answers and approval signatures are automatically captured to provide a complete audit trail.

Checklists

Customise your checklists for each type of collateral and approval stage to ensure specific checks are completed and store all responses automatically.

Asset library

Store and share any type of file within the central asset library.

Issue logging

Keep track of the reasons why approvals are rejected to monitor trends and implement continual improvements.

Calendars

Compliance expiry dates, project tasks and timescales are automatically updated in the calendar views.

Automated reminders

Manage scheduling with automated email reminders when review deadlines are approaching or missed and when compliance expiry dates are reached.

Electronic storage

Store all of your collateral including videos, websites, blogs, emails, Twitter messages, LinkedIn profiles, PDF’s, Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint files, JPEGs and GIFs electronically.

Website capture

Capture and store snapshots of your website by emailing the URL to the relevant job bag. This ensures a record of your website is retained for audit purposes even when the site is no longer publically available.

Reporting

Create management reports on any data that you capture which can be viewed on screen or exported into Excel or PDF formats.

User management

Control user access, approval authority, briefing form fields and create workflow sequences.

Search facility

Search across all sites and within documents to find content instantly.

24/7 access

Accessible from any internet enabled PC, laptop or iPad providing 24/7 global access.

Securely hosted

Online and offline encryption to ensure your data and assets are secure.

Optional features

DocGen - Automated publishing

Instantly publish a range of branded documents such as factsheets, sales aids and PowerPoint presentations using approved content and data.

Social Toolbar – Twitter management

Manage all your corporate Twitter accounts from one central dashboard. View timelines, search, monitor mentions and direct messages and ensure all Tweets are approved before they are posted.

OnBrand - Microsoft templates

Our OnBrand templates work with all versions of Microsoft office and include custom ribbon features for controlling layout, styles, fonts and colour palettes. Additional tools are available for managing image libraries and instantly generating branded tables, charts and graphs.

SmartShare - Word content library

The SmartShare content library is available as a plugin for Microsoft Word. It gives document authors the ability to search libraries of pre-approved content like staff biographies, photos, charts, policy wording or proven business winning text, which can be inserted directly into their documents.

RightSlide PowerPoint slide library

The RightSlide template and slide library is available as a plugin for Microsoft PowerPoint. It enables users to create presentations in your corporate style and browse and insert pre-approved slides, charts, images and videos. New or edited slides contain a “Draft” watermark to easily identify slides that require approval.

Old presentations and slides are instantly updated with the latest versions so you know the most up-to-date content is used.
